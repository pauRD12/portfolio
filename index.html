    About Me

    Graduated in engineering, my fascination with visual effects led me to complete the Houdini Master's program at Planeta CG while working as a teacher at an institute. Following a rewarding initial experience as an FX Intern at Framestore, contributing to the "Mickey 17" project, I'm actively seeking new challenges. My interest increasingly lies in technical roles within VFX, hence I currently dedicate my time to refining my skills in Python and taking my first steps in machine learning.

    While I reside in Barcelona, I also hold a work permit for Canada and am willing to relocate anywhere to pursue my dream of professional development in something I'm passionate about. I'm a hardworking individual with a great capacity for adaptation who enjoys teamwork. I'm always ready to face new challenges and learn new things. I don't fear the constant changes in this industry; instead, I see them as opportunities for growth and professional development.
